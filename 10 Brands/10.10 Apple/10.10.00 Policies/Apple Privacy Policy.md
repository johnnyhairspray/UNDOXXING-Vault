# Apple Privacy Policy

**Source:** https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/en-ww/
**Last Updated by Apple:** July 30, 2025

---

## What Is Personal Data at Apple?

Apple treats any data that relates to an identified or identifiable individual or that is linked or linkable to them by Apple as "personal data," regardless of where the individual lives. Includes data that directly identifies you (name) and data that can reasonably be used to identify you (device serial number). Aggregated data is considered non-personal data.

Covers Apple websites, Apple apps (Music, Wallet, etc.), and in-person interactions (phone, Apple Store visits).

---

## Your Privacy Rights at Apple

- Know, access, correct, transfer, restrict processing of, and delete personal data
- Right not to be discriminated against for exercising privacy rights
- Right to withdraw consent at any time
- Right to lodge a complaint with applicable regulator

Exercise at: privacy.apple.com | California: 1-800-275-2273

Exceptions: Apple may deny deletion requests where legally required to retain data, where deletion would undermine anti-fraud purposes, or where requests are frivolous.

## Personal Data Apple Collects from You

Apple states it collects only what it needs. Categories:

- **Account Information** — Apple Account details, email, registered devices, account status, age
- **Device Information** — Serial number, browser type, device identifiers
- **Contact Information** — Name, email, physical address, phone
- **Payment Information** — Billing address, bank details, credit/debit card data
- **Transaction Information** — App Store and Apple service purchases
- **Fraud Prevention Information** — Device trust score
- **Usage Data** — App launches, browsing/search history, product interaction, crash/diagnostic data
- **Location Information** — Precise (Find My, region services with consent); coarse generally
- **Health Information** — Physical/mental health data, health condition inferences
- **Fitness Information** — Exercise data shared voluntarily
- **Financial Information** — Salary, income, assets; Apple Card/Cash data
- **Government ID Data** — Certain jurisdictions; wireless setup, credit extension, reservations
- **Communications Content** — Customer support interactions, social media contacts

---

## Personal Data Apple Receives from Other Sources

- **Individuals** — Data sent by others (gifts, invitations, shared content)
- **At Your Direction** — Carrier data, loyalty programs at user direction
- **Apple Partners** — Third-party verification for security/fraud prevention

**Applebot:** Web crawler collecting publicly available data to power search and foundational AI models. Apple states it does not connect crawled data to individual users or build individual profiles. Private personal data not used to train foundational AI models. Opt-out via Apple Intelligence Privacy Inquiries form.

**Research Datasets:** Apple may use datasets containing images, voices, or other potentially identifiable data for R&D, in compliance with applicable law. Apple states it does not attempt to reidentify individuals.

## Apple's Use of Personal Data

Apple states it uses personal data only with a valid legal basis (contract necessity, consent, legal obligation, legitimate interests). Uses:

- **Power Services** — Service provision, internal auditing, troubleshooting
- **Process Transactions** — Name, purchase, payment data
- **Communicate** — Transactions, account, marketing, important notices (cannot opt out of critical notices)
- **Security and Fraud Prevention** — Loss prevention, CSAM scanning
- **Personalization** — Where user opts in
- **Comply with Law** — Tax, reporting, lawful government requests

Apple states it does not use algorithms or profiling to make decisions significantly affecting users without human review. Does not use/disclose sensitive personal data for purposes requiring California opt-out.

**Retention:** Personal data retained only as long as necessary to fulfill collection purposes or as required by law.

---

## Apple's Sharing of Personal Data

- **Service Providers** — Contractually prohibited from using data for own purposes
- **Partners** — Apple Card (Goldman Sachs), Apple Cash (Green Dot), other service partners
- **Developers/Publishers** — Subscriber IDs (unique per user per developer) for subscription reporting; resets 180 days after all subscriptions cancelled
- **Others at Direction** — Carrier activation, etc.
- **Law/Security** — National security, law enforcement, public importance; terms enforcement; reorganization/merger/sale

**Apple states it does not sell personal data** (including under Nevada and California definitions). Apple states it does not "share" personal data as defined under California law.

---

## Cookies and Other Technologies

- **Communications Cookies** — Network traffic, error detection
- **Strictly Necessary Cookies** — Site formatting, authentication, cart
- **Other Cookies** — User behavior analytics, ad effectiveness, browsing preferences

Apple generally treats cookie-collected data as non-personal data except where local law treats IP addresses as personal. Combined data treated as personal data.

**Apple Advertising Platform:** Appears on App Store, Apple News, Stocks, Apple TV. Apple states platform does not track users across apps/sites and does not share data with data brokers. Personalized Ads can be disabled in Settings.

---

## Transfer of Personal Data Between Countries

Data may be transferred globally to Apple-affiliated companies. EU/UK/Switzerland data controlled by Apple Distribution International Limited in Ireland under Standard Contractual Clauses. Generally stored by Apple Inc. in the United States. China data per Personal Information Protection Law.

Apple holds Global CBPR and Global PRP System certifications.
